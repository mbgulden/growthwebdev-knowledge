**DOCUMENT 1: WEBSITE CONTENT GATHERING GUIDE (OPEN KNOWLEDGE FORMAT)**

### **Welcome\! Let’s Build Your Website Content**

To build your new high-performance website, we use a system called **Open Knowledge Format (OKF)**. Instead of writing long, messy pages, we break your business down into individual "Knowledge Documents." This allows our AI system to perfectly organize, style, and link your information together for the best possible user experience.  
Since you are gathering your content today, please create **separate sections, separate files, or clear headings** for each of the 5 documents outlined below. Simply write out the answers in plain text or bullet points.

### **Document 1: Business Core & Mission**

*File Name Suggestion: company\_profile*  
This document establishes your core identity and explains why your training center exists.

* **Official Business Name:** \[e.g., Meridian Women’s Defense Academy\]  
* **Core Mission Statement:** (In 1–2 sentences, what is your ultimate goal for the women who train with you?)  
* **The "Why Us" (Unique Selling Proposition):** Why should a woman choose your classes over a standard co-ed gun range or general self-defense class? What makes your environment safe, empowering, and different?  
* **Core Values:** (List 3–5 words or principles that guide how you teach, such as Empowerment, Safety, Confidence, Community).

### **Document 2: Class & Training Directory**

*File Name Suggestion: Create a separate entry for each class you offer (e.g., class\_concealed\_carry, class\_basic\_handgun, class\_self\_defense)*  
For **each** class, workshop, or private training package you offer, please provide the following details:

* **Class Title:** \[e.g., Idaho Enhanced Concealed Carry for Women\]  
* **High-Level Summary:** (A 1-paragraph description of what the class is and who it is for.)  
* **What You Will Learn:** (Provide 4–6 bullet points detailing the specific skills covered—e.g., situational awareness, proper grip and stance, Idaho firearm laws, handling recoil.)  
* **Class Prerequisites:** (Does a student need prior experience, or is this beginner-friendly?)  
* **What to Bring / Gear Required:** (Do they need their own firearm, ammunition, ear/eye protection, or do you provide rentals?)  
* **Duration & Price:** \[e.g., 4 Hours, $125\]  
* **Call to Action:** (Where do they go next? e.g., "Click here to view upcoming dates and reserve your spot.")

### **Document 3: Instructor & Partner Profiles**

*File Name Suggestion: Create one for each partner/instructor (e.g., instructor\_jane\_doe)*  
People buy from people they trust, especially when it comes to personal safety. Please provide a profile for each partner and instructor.

* **Full Name & Role:** \[e.g., Jane Doe, Co-Founder & Lead Firearms Instructor\]  
* **Professional Background & Certifications:** (List your NRA certifications, law enforcement/military background, martial arts ranks, or relevant training hours.)  
* **Personal Teaching Philosophy:** (Why do you love teaching self-defense specifically to women? What is your approach to helping students overcome fear or anxiety around firearms?)  
* **A Personal Note/Quote:** (A direct quote from you to a prospective student. e.g., *"My goal isn't just to teach you how to shoot; it's to make sure you walk to your car at night knowing you can protect yourself."*)  
* **Headshot Placeholder:** (Note the filename of the high-resolution photo you will use for this profile.)

### **Document 4: FAQs & Local Idaho Logistics**

*File Name Suggestion: faq\_and\_logistics*  
Anticipate the questions your students are asking before they sign up.

* **Top 5 Customer Questions:** (Write out the 5 questions you hear most often from prospective students, along with your clear answers. *Hint: Include questions about being nervous, never holding a gun before, or physical limitations for self-defense.*)  
* **Idaho CCW Specifics:** (Briefly explain the practical difference between standard and Enhanced Concealed Carry in Idaho so out-of-state newcomers understand what they need.)

### **Document 5: Location, Hours, & Contact Operations**

*File Name Suggestion: location\_ops*  
Where and when can people find you?

* **Classroom/Range Location(s):** (Physical addresses where classes are held in or around Meridian, ID. If you use a specific local range, name it here.)  
* **Business Contact Info:** (The public phone number and email address you want displayed on the site.)  
* **Operating Hours:** (When are you available for inquiries or private bookings?)  
* **Social Media Links:** (Links to your business Facebook, Instagram, or YouTube if applicable.)
