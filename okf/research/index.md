## What counts as "research"

Reference material gathered to inform product, SEO, or technical
decisions. Not a one-time report or audit — research is re-read across
many projects over time.

A research doc is *not*:
- A project-specific audit (those go in `okf/audits/` or project spokes)
- An incident report (those go in `okf/reports/`)
- An architecture decision (those go in `okf/decisions/`)
